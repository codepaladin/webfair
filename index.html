Webfair is a live virtual event on a mission to grant everybody, regardless of income, location, or renown, the opportunity to both watch and give quality presentations.

We do this by removing all fees and democratizing the speaking process. Half of Webfair's panels are user submitted and community voted. You get a say in who speaks and what's heard.

Follow @webfair for updates and announcments, and please contact us with any questions/comments.
