About Us

WEBFAIR is a live event brought to you by Codepen.io. Our goal is to bring a live event to the tech and web community at large - not just the community that can travel and pay.

In addition to making the conference free, we're democratizing the speaking process by allowing you, the audience, to submit your panel ideas to a community vote.

Follow @webfair for updates and announcements.
