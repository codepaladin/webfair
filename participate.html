Who Can Speak

Anybody! We're all experts at something and Webfair encourages everybody to speak up.

What Topics

Like Hacker News, we welcome you to submit anything the audience would find interesting. The audience is primarily in the tech and startup community, so please keep that in mind.

20 Minutes

Human attention spans last around 20 minutes, so that's your speaking limit. There will also be around 10 minutes for Q&A after presentations.

Preperation

We're all professional here, so we expect speeches to of high quality. If you're speech is voted, we will work with you on how to make it just right.
